Ice-Cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from a mixture of cream,sugar ,milk and flavorings that is chilled and churned. It originated thousands of years ago as flavored snow and evolved in Italy before spreading to the rest of the world. Modern ice cream is produced in factories using large machines and comes in many varieties, such as frozen yogurt, sorbet, and non-dairy options.

How to make a Ice-Cream Step-by-Step❓❓
  1. Put milk and sugar in large bag and seal add vanilla or cocoa for flavour and choc chip.
  2. Put the ive and salt in small bag.
  3. Put the small bag inside large bag.
  4. Shoke the bag continuosly for 10 min until mixed together and frozen
  5. After shaking your Ice-Cream is ready to eat!!
