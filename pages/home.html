2.ICE-CREAM🍦

Ice cream is a popular frozen dessert typically made from dairy products like milk or cream, sweetened with sugar, and flavored with ingredients such as vanilla, chocolate, or fruit. Its production involves freezing a mixture while incorporating air to create a smooth texture, and it has a long history dating back to ancient times, becoming widely available with modern refrigeration and industrial production.
